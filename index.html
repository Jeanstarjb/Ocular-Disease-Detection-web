Our Mission

At the Institute for Ocular Vision & Research, we are committed to advancing the understanding and treatment of eye diseases through cutting-edge research and compassionate patient care.

Our multidisciplinary team of researchers, clinicians, and innovators work tirelessly to translate scientific discoveries into life-changing treatments that preserve and restore vision for patients worldwide.

Our Core Focus

We concentrate our research efforts on the most pressing challenges in ocular health

Glaucoma & Nerve Protection

Developing innovative neuroprotective therapies to preserve optic nerve function and prevent vision loss in glaucoma patients.

Learn More →

Macular Degeneration

Researching cellular regeneration techniques and gene therapies to combat age-related macular degeneration and restore central vision.

Learn More →

AI in Diagnostics

Leveraging artificial intelligence and machine learning to enable earlier detection and more precise diagnosis of retinal diseases.
